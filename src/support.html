Figma Copy and Paste Issues

There is sometimes consistency problems with copying and pasting the pre-built design prototypes. Luckily, there's an easy fix!

If you copy and paste an element and find the connections (blue lines) disappear when you paste the new element simply change to the Prototype tab (Option/Alt + 9) and copy and paste the element again.
