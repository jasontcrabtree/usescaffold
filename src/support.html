Support

General Issues

For general or unmentioned issues please send me an email via jasontcrabtree.com. and I'll do my best to respond ASAP.

Figma Copy and Paste Issues

There is sometimes consistency problems with copying and pasting the pre-built design prototypes. Luckily, there's an easy fix!

If you copy and paste a design and the prototype connections (blue lines) disappear when you paste the copied design change to the Prototype tab Option / Alt + 9 and copy and paste the element again.

File Access and Lost License

If you lose access to your Scaffold Library files or license please send an email to email and I'll see how I can help.

Refunds
