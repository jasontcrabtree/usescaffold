License Details

Lifetime License Details

The Scaffold Library is sold as 1.0 of a lifetime license. By buying Scaffold, you get lifetime access and downloads to Scaffold 1.0, plus any updates between April 2020 and Dec 2020. Future releases, if any, will be released under a new version number.

This license structure is similar to Sketch and Flowkit , two other software downloads released under similar licenses.

Multiple and Team Licenses

If you need multiple licenses for your team, you'll need to buy a team license. If you would like more than five licenses, please send an email to jasontcrabtree@gmail.com.

Legal License: (EULA)

The End-User License Agreement (EULA) is a legal document that protects me, and you. Read the End-User License Agreement (EULA).
